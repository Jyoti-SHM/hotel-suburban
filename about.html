Your Comfort-First Hotel & Banquet Destination

Hotel Suburban offers more than just a stay — it’s a space where comfort meets celebration. Conveniently located on Chandigarh-Baddi Road, our hotel is thoughtfully designed for travelers seeking peace, convenience, and modern amenities.

Whether you're attending a special event or planning one yourself, our elegant banquet space is ideal for weddings, corporate meetings, and private functions. Surrounded by natural beauty and backed by attentive service, Hotel Suburban is where meaningful moments are created and cherished.

Hotel’S Facilities

At Hotel Suburban, we believe in keeping things simple, comfortable, and convenient. From clean, well-appointed rooms to warm hospitality, we’re here to make your stay easy and enjoyable. We also welcome pets — because every member of your family deserves to travel (pet charges apply).
